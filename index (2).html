FAQ

Are the Diamonds and Gold Real?

All Jewelry are Certified by GIA and IGI Labs that have been Grading Loose Diamonds and Jewelry for Several Decades.

What is Different about Akaris Jewelry?

Akaris Jewelry stands out due to our Unique Diamond Choices and the Ability to give our Customers a View into the world of Diamonds with over 100+ Different and Unique Diamond Shapes and Colours to Choose from for their Personalised Jewelry Piece.

Do you Offer Unique Designs?

We believe that each person has a Unique Jewelry Personality — not one Design suits everyone. Thus, we Create Pieces we Believe Represent the Person and who they are.

What is the Process?

A Dedicated Associate will Assist the Client from Consultation to Creating the Design that Represents the Client, to Selecting the Right Materials, to Quality Assurance of the Jewelry Several Times until Perfection is Achieved and Delivered. Your Associate will always be available for your Next Visit and will Update you whenever a Once in a Thousand Diamond is Sourced — ensuring Exclusive First Viewing.

How long does it take for Jewelry to be Finished?

3 to 6 Weeks Depending on the Design Complexity and Availability of Rare Diamonds.

Yes, we have Ready Made pieces like Solitaire Rings, Earrings, and More — Timeless pieces that can be worn on any Occasion and will always look Elegant and Beautiful.
